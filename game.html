How Do I Make Games

I mainly use Unity Engine, while some basic modelling is done in Blender. I edit textures through Photoshop [if I need to], and audios are pitched-down/up samples from various free things I found on the internet, or recorded with my phone [walking, wind noises etc.].
Coding is done through Blueprints/C++/C#.
