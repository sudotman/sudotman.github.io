Game Design

How Do I Make Games

I mainly make games in Unity Game Engine, while some basic modelling is done in Blender. I edit textures through Photoshop (if I need to), and audios are pitched-down/up samples from various free things I found on the internet, or recorded with my phone (walking, wind noises etc.).

Shiva

A 3D Hack-n-Slash/RPG game I developed. I am a very avid reader of mythology, I have read a lot of puranas, itihasa. Shiva has a lot of different occurences that has happened to him and a lot of material is present that I always wanted to explore. This is my attempt at that. Made in Unity URP.
